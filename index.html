Alberta with THE BOYS - Banff, Lakes & Memories

5 min read·Summer 2024 · Banff, Alberta

Some trips exist beyond the photos. The Alberta trip with my boys was one of those. No agenda, no stress - just mountains, glaciers, open roads, and the kind of laughter that makes your stomach hurt.

We drove into Banff on a sunny morning and it felt like stepping into a painting. The turquoise lakes - Louise, Moraine, Peyto looked too perfect to be real.

Highlights: the Banff Gondola, the Columbia Icefield, the Skywalk, kayaking, a movie night under the stars, and long drives on Highway 93 with the best views and better company.
